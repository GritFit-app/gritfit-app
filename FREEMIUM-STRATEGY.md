# GritFit Freemium Strategy

## Overview

**Free Version:** Essential workout tracking (bare bones)  
**Paid Version:** Premium features + AI + unlimited everything  
**Pricing:** $9.99/month or $79.99/year (33% savings)

---

## FREE VERSION

### What's Included (Bare Bones)

**Workout Tracking:**
- ✅ Log exercises, sets, reps, weight
- ✅ Basic workout history (last 30 days)
- ✅ Simple rest timer (60s fixed)
- ✅ 3 saved workouts max

**Basic Stats:**
- ✅ Total workouts counter
- ✅ Current streak
- ✅ Weekly volume (current week only)

**Limited Features:**
- ✅ 20 exercise library
- ✅ No plate calculator
- ✅ No progress photos
- ✅ No achievements
- ✅ Ads (banner at bottom)

**Restrictions:**
- ❌ No data export
- ❌ No backup/sync
- ❌ No social features
- ❌ No AI recommendations
- ❌ Limited to 3 workouts/month creation

---

## PAID VERSION ($9.99/month)

### Premium Features (All Bells & Whistles)

**Advanced Tracking:**
- ✅ Unlimited workouts
- ✅ Unlimited exercise library (500+)
- ✅ Custom rest timer (any duration)
- ✅ Superset & drop set tracking
- ✅ RPE tracking
- ✅ Warm-up calculator
- ✅ Notes per exercise

**Progress Tools:**
- ✅ Progress photos (unlimited)
- ✅ Photo comparison (side-by-side)
- ✅ Weight tracking graphs
- ✅ Measurements log (chest, arms, waist, etc.)
- ✅ Body fat % tracking
- ✅ Export data (CSV/PDF)

**Calculators & Tools:**
- ✅ Plate calculator (visual + math)
- ✅ 1RM calculator (multiple formulas)
- ✅ Wilks/GLP score calculator
- ✅ Volume load calculator
- ✅ Warm-up sets auto-generator

**Gamification:**
- ✅ 50+ achievements & badges
- ✅ Leaderboards (global & friends)
- ✅ Challenges & competitions
- ✅ Streak rewards
- ✅ Level system (XP gains)

**Social Features:**
- ✅ Friends list
- ✅ Share workouts
- ✅ Comment on friend workouts
- ✅ Private groups
- ✅ Coach mode (train clients)

**AI Features:**
- ✅ Smart workout suggestions
- ✅ Plateau detection
- ✅ Exercise substitution recommendations
- ✅ Form tips per exercise
- ✅ Auto-progression (suggest weight increases)

**Data & Sync:**
- ✅ Cloud backup
- ✅ Sync across devices
- ✅ Apple Health / Google Fit sync
- ✅ Wearable integration (Apple Watch, Fitbit)
- ✅ Data export (CSV, PDF, Excel)

**Customization:**
- ✅ Dark/Light theme
- ✅ Custom color schemes
- ✅ Widgets for home screen
- ✅ Custom exercise creation
- ✅ Custom workout templates

**No Ads:**
- ✅ Completely ad-free experience

## PRICING STRATEGY

### Monthly: $9.99/month
- Full access to all premium features
- Cancel anytime
- Best for: Trying it out

### Yearly: $79.99/year
- Same features, 33% discount
- Works out to $6.67/month
- Best for: Committed users

### Lifetime: $199 one-time
- Pay once, own forever
- All future updates included
- Best for: Hardcore lifters

## COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE

**vs. Strong (Free):**
- Strong charges $4.99/month for basic features
- GritFit free is more generous
- GritFit Premium has AI (Strong doesn't)

**vs. Hevy (Free):**
- Hevy limits to 4 routines free
- GritFit allows 3 workouts (similar)
- GritFit has better design & gamification

**vs. JEFIT (Free):**
- JEFIT has ads + limited features
- GritFit cleaner UI
- GritFit better community features

**Our Edge:**
- Better design (dark mode + glow effects)
- AI features (competitors don't have)
- Gamification (achievements + levels)
- Social features (friends + leaderboards)
