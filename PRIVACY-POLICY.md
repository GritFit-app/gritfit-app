# GritFit Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 20, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

GritFit ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our fitness tracking application.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Email address, username, password, profile photo
- **Fitness Data:** Workout logs, exercises performed, weights, reps, sets, duration
- **Body Metrics:** Weight, height, progress photos (optional)
- **Preferences:** Fitness goals, equipment available, experience level

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, app version
- **Usage Data:** Features used, time spent in app, crash reports
- **Location Data:** Only if you enable location services for gym check-ins
- **IP Address:** For security and analytics purposes

### 2.3 Health and Fitness Data
We collect sensitive health information including:
- Workout history and performance data
- Exercise types and intensity
- Progress photos (if you choose to upload them)
- Training volume and frequency

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Uses
- **Provide Services:** Track workouts, calculate analytics, generate AI recommendations
- **Personalization:** Tailor workout suggestions to your goals and progress
- **Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve app features
- **Communication:** Send workout reminders, progress updates, and account notifications

### 3.2 AI and Analytics
- Your workout data is used to train our AI recommendation engine
- Data is anonymized for aggregate analytics
- We use patterns to improve workout suggestion algorithms

### 3.3 Marketing (with consent)
- We may send promotional emails about new features
- You can opt out of marketing communications at any time
- We never sell your data to third parties for marketing

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage
- **Free Users:** Data stored locally on your device
- **Premium Users:** Data synced to secure cloud servers (US-based)
- **Backups:** Regular encrypted backups for Premium users

### 4.2 Security Measures
- Industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and authentication requirements

### 4.3 Data Retention
- Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days of deletion
- Legal requirements: Some data may be retained as required by law

---

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### 5.2 Service Providers
We may share data with:
- **Stripe:** For payment processing (Premium subscriptions)
- **Cloud Hosting:** For data storage and backup
- **Analytics:** For app improvement (anonymized data only)

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights or property
- Prevent fraud or abuse
- Protect user safety

### 5.4 Business Transfers
If GritFit is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to the same privacy protections.

---

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Control
You can:
- View all data associated with your account
- Download your data (GDPR data portability)
- Update or correct your information
- Delete your account and data

### 6.2 Data Portability
Premium users can export their complete workout history in CSV or JSON format.

### 6.3 Opt-Out Options
- Marketing emails: Unsubscribe link in every email
- Analytics: Disable in app settings
- Location tracking: Disable in device settings
